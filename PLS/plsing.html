Science degrees project

Illustration of the project

The "Scientific Degrees" project in collaboration with the University of Potenza focuses on the application of graph concepts in various scientific fields. During my collaboration with the project, I took care of the graphical part of the presentation, creating engaging and informative visual representations of graph concepts. The presentation is designed to clearly and captivatingly illustrate the properties and algorithms of graphs, as well as their applications in practical contexts. Using colors, shapes, and diagrams, I worked to convey complex concepts in an accessible way, allowing audiences to easily understand the potential and implications of graphs in science. Graphical representations have included detailed diagrams of graphs and subgraphs, dynamic visualizations of search algorithms and paths, as well as examples of graph applications in optimization problems, social networks, transportation, and more. My attention to the graphical portion of the presentation helped make the educational material engaging and easy to understand, providing viewers with a solid foundation to further explore the world of graphs and their scientific applications.
